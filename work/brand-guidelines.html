Fresh Brand Guidelines

At Fresh Tilled Soil, we evolved the brand over the past year as we redesigned the new website. We expanded upon Mark Grambau's initial brand redesign, iterating on a few aesthetic pieces and defining specific rules for usage. I took on the task of designing and coding an internal brand guidelines microsite to serve as a resource for anything Fresh-branded. It's a work in progress, so there are more sections being added every month.
