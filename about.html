More about me

I'm a product designer at Fresh Tilled Soil, a digital product strategy and design agency. I'm also a recent graduate of Northeastern University, where I cofounded and designed for NU's one and only design studio, Scout. I combine a passion for design thinking strategies with a respect for data/research to create human-centered, intentional, beautiful experiences.

My main interests lie in strategy, user experience design, and user interface design, but I often dabble in front-end development and print design on the side. I spend a lot of time practicing my French (conversational now!), climbing (and falling), going to McDonald's, and making origami ninja stars.
